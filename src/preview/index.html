AP Automation
Modernize your financial operations

Free up your finance teams from the day-to-day inefficiencies of manual accounts payable, and working with point systems. Tipalti delivers end to end payables solutions, purpose-built to handle the evolving needs of your business—with a focus on scale, efficiency, controls, and visibility.

Learn about AP Automation
  • Scale rapidly without adding resources
  • Eliminate payables workload and focus on high-value initiatives
  • Strengthen financial controls and reduce compliance risk
  • Accelerate financial close and get real-time spend insight
  • Stop manual invoice processes Touchless invoice processing with machine learning, advanced approvals, and PO matching.
  • Easily pay suppliers in minutes Execute payments in a wide range of methods and currencies all around the globe, manage payment approvals, built-in fraud detection, and instant payment.
  • Streamline your global workflow Unify global operations and ensure compliance across all entities and subsidiaries with HQ view.
  • Stay compliant and reduce risk Enterprise-grade financial controls with role-based security, audit logs, and ensure compliance with KPMG approved tax engine.
  • Focus on value added tasks Empower supplier self service and reduce inquiries with a multi-language portal with supplier self service, and real-time view of payments and invoices.

PO MANAGEMENT
Reduce the risk of maverick spend

Finally, your finance team can have greater visibility and control over corporate spending. We help streamline purchase requests, approvals, vendor selection, PO creation with real-time budget visibility.

Learn about PO MANAGEMENT
  • Get control and visibility over your company’s purchase orders
  • Standardize and streamline purchase requests and approvals
  • Simplify with automatic PO creation & ERP sync
  • Bring corporate spend under management With simple, predefined custom forms, employees can easily submit purchase requests ensuring all critical spend information is collected upfront.
  • Put an end to purchase order chaos Eliminate messy emails, spreadsheets, and tickets by managing every aspect of your procurement in one centralized place.
  • Gain visibility and control Keep all procurement data easily searchable and accessible. With access to the true spend pipeline, finance, procurement, legal and security can get involved early.
TIPALTI CARD
Control Spend, Increase Visibility, and Receive Cash-Back

Gain full control and visibility over your corporate card spend with automatic reconciliation with ERP systems. The Tipalti virtual card is a simple, convenient, flexible, and secure card solution providing extended working capital benefits with cash-back rebates.

Learn about TIPALTI CARD
  • Drive adoption with an employee-friendly payment option
  • Control spend and reduce risk via card settings and workflows
  • Get cash-back on all card transactions
  • Centralize spend in a single location regardless of payment type
  • Automatic reconciliation removes the headaches of manual reconciliation
  • Eliminate Rogue Spend Provide employees with a payment option that is easy to use.
  • Easily Set Up Virtual Cards for Employees in Minutes Set up, manage, and delete cards with ease.
  • Stay Compliant and Reduce Fraud Issue cards with robust security and financial controls such as spend limits, vendor limits, and pre-approved categories.
  • Earn Cash-Back Get cash-back for every dollar you spend via virtual payment cards. It’s that simple!
  • Centralize Spend and Improve Visibility Manage all your company’s spend in one place with visibility that enables informed decisions about when to change or remove access to digital cards.
  • Automatic Reconciliation Say goodbye to manual, spreadsheet-based reconciliation processes. Virtual card transactions are automatically captured and processed like any other payment method.
